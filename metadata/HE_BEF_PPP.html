Direct source

Data for this indicator are a mix of calculations and imports from original sources.

Source 1: OECD Research and Development Statistics, Gross domestic expenditure on R-D (GERD) by sector of performance and source of funds.

Source 2: Calculations based on Eurostat, Science, Technology and Innovation Databases, Research and Development Database. For countries for which data are available and that are not already covered in the first source.

Source Periodicity

As new data become available.

Source metadata

Source 1:
OECD (2002), Proposed Standard Practice for Surveys of Research and Experimental Development, "Frascati Manual", http://www.oecd.org/sti/frascati-manual-2015-9789264239012-en.htm.

OECD R&D Sources and Methods Database.

Source 2: Eurostat reference metadata in Euro SDMX Metadata Structure (ESMS) for R&D and GBAORD data.

Geographic coverage

Source 1: 35 OECD countries, Argentina, China, Romania, Russian Federation, Singapore, South Africa, and Chinese Taipei.

Source 2: 28 EU Member States, EFTA (Iceland, Liechtenstein, Norway and Switzerland) and Candidate (the FYR of Macedonia and Turkey) countries, and China, Japan, Korea, Russian Federation, United States.
