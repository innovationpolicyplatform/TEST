Direct source

Data for this indicator are a mix of calculations and imports from original sources.

Source 1: OECD Main Science and Technology Indicators (MSTI) Database, www.oecd.org/sti/msti.

Source 2: Calculations based on Eurostat, Science, Technology and Innovation Databases, Research and Development Database. For countries for which data are available and that are not already covered in the first source.

Source 3: Calculations based on UNESCO Institute for Statistics (UIS), Science, Technology and Innovation Databases, Research and Development Database. For the rest of the world.

Source Periodicity

Source 1: Bi-annual in January and June. MSTI indicators cover the resources devoted to research and development, patent families, technology balance of payments and international trade in R&D-intensive industries from 1981 onwards,

Source metadata

Source 1: OECD Main S&T Indicators Database, statistical content and documentation (http://www.oecd.org/sti/msti.htm).
OECD Patent Statistics Manual, www.oecd.org/innovation/inno/oecdpatentstatisticsmanual.htm.

Proposed Standard Practice for Surveys of Research and Experimental Development, "Frascati Manual", www.oecd.org/sti/frascatimanual.
OECD (2005), Measuring Globalisation: OECD Handbook on Economic Globalisation Indicators, OECD Publishing, Paris, www.oecd.org/science/sci-tech/oecdhandbookoneconomicglobalisationindicators.htm.



Source 2: Eurostat reference metadata in Euro SDMX Metadata Structure (ESMS) for R&D and GBAORD data.

Source 3: Background information on R&D statistics in the UIS Data Centre (pdf).
