Direct source

Data for this indicator are drawn from Global Entrepreuneruship Monitor Global Entrepreuneruship Monitor

Source Periodicity

Data are based on surveys on entrepreneurship perception of individiduals and national experts on entrepreneurship. Percentage of 18-64 population (individuals involved in any stage of entrepreneurial activity excluded) who indicate perceived opportunities

Source metadata

The GEM Adult Population Survey (APS) measures the level and nature of entrepreneurial activity around the world. It is administered to a representative national sample of at least 2000 respondents.

Periodicity

Annual.
